Wings of Fire

by arun thwari

"Wings of Fire" is an autobiography written by Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, the former President
of India and renowned scientist. The book offers a captivating glimpse into his childhood and
early adult life, growing up in a humble background in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. It chronicles his
relentless pursuit of knowledge, which led him to become a prominent aerospace engineer, instrumental
in India's missile and space programs. Kalam's journey from a small village to the Rashtrapati Bhavan,
India's presidential residence, is a tale of resilience, dedication, and unwavering commitment to the
betterment of his nation. "Wings of Fire" is not just a biography but also a source of inspiration
for countless individuals, especially aspiring scientists and young Indians, who aspire to reach for the stars.

The 3 mistake of my life

Indian author by chetan bhagat

"The 3 Mistakes of My Life" is a popular novel written by renowned Indian author Chetan Bhagat.
This book delves into the lives of three friends in the city of Ahmedabad, India, as they navigate
through personal and societal challenges. Through a compelling narrative, the author explores
themes of friendship, love, ambition, and the consequences of the titular 'mistakes.' Bhagat's
storytelling prowess and relatable characters make this novel a thought-provoking and engaging read
that resonates with readers of various age groups.

Harry poter

and the sorcerers stone by j.k.rowthing

"Harry Potter" is a world-renowned fantasy book series penned by the British author J.K. Rowling.
The series follows the adventures of a young wizard named Harry Potter as he discovers his magical
heritage and embarks on a journey to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Set in a richly
imagined magical world, the books explore themes of friendship, courage, and the eternal struggle
between good and evil. With its vivid characters, captivating storyline, and intricate world-building,
the "Harry Potter" series has captivated readers of all ages and has become a beloved classic in the
realm of literature, inspiring a successful film series and a dedicated fan following worldwide.
